Beginner Plan 12 weeks

Legs

  • squats 4x10
  • deadlifts 4x8
  • barbell lunges 3x10 (each leg)
  • leg extensions super set with leg curls 3x12 (each exercise)

Chest/Triceps

  • dumbbell bench press 4x10
  • dumbbell incline bench 3x12
  • dumbbell flyes 3x10
  • tricep bench dips 1x30 (take breaks as needed)
  • overhead dumbbell tricep extension 3x12
  • cable (use rope attachment) tricep pushdown 4x8

Back/Bis

  • assisted pullups 1x20 (take breaks as needed)
  • narrow grip cable seated rows 3x12
  • lat pulldowns 3x12
  • alternating dumbbell curls 3x10 (each arm)
  • barbell curls 4x8
  • preacher curls 3x10

Shoulders/Abs/Calves

  • seated dumbbell press 3x12
  • dumbbell front raises superset with dumbbell side raises 3x10 (each exercise)
  • seated calf raises 3x15
  • standing calf raises using smith machine 3x15
  • bicycle crunches 3x16
  • lying leg raises 3x12

Rest

you will have 3 rest days. you can take them consecutively or have a rest day in between each workout (which is what we recommend).


notes:
  • set/rep convention: 3x10 means 3 sets of 10 reps each with a 45 second break in between each set
  • superset: two exercises performed consecutively without a break in between.
