Advanced Plan 8 weeks

Chest/Triceps

  • dumbbell or barbell flat bench (alternate each week) 5x8
  • dumbbell or barbell incline bench (alternate each week) 4x10
  • decline bench 4x8-10
  • dips to failure superset with chest flyes (cable machine or dumbbells) 3x12
  • tricep skullcrushers 30 method
  • cable extension with rope or ez bar 4x10-12
  • dumbbell overhead extensions 3x10 super set with dumbbell kickbacks to failure

Back/Biceps

  • pullups 5 sets to failure (alternate grips)
  • deadlifts warm-up with 20,10,8 then 4x6
  • wide grip lat pull down 4x8
  • bent over dumbbell rows 4x10
  • reverse grip lat pulldowns 3x8, 15
  • close grip rows (machine, cable, or bent over) 3x10, drop weight to failure
  • dumbbell zottoman curls (each arm) 3x8
  • straight bar 21 method
  • dumbbell preacher curls (open chest, eliminate shoulder) 3x8-12
  • hammer curls with plate 3xfailure (around 12-15 reps)

Legs

  • squats warm-up with 20, 15, 10 then 5x6-8
  • leg press or front squat (alternate each week) 4x12-15
  • walking lunges with dumbbells or barbell 3x10 (each leg), as many unweighted reps as possible for 1 minute
  • leg extensions 8,10,15,20 superset with leg curls 20,15,10,8
  • dumbbell or barbell romanian deadlifts 15,12,10,8

Shoulders/Calves

  • dumbbell or barbell press (alternate each week) warm-up with 20,15,10 then 4x8
  • upright rows 4x8
  • behind the head barbell press (seated or standing) 4x10-12
  • side raises superset with rear delt flyes on chest fly machine 3x12-15
  • plate raises superset 3xfailure with seated calf raises 3x20
  • standing calf raises 3x20
  • donkey calf raises 3x20

Biceps/Triceps

alternate between week a and week b

week a
  • note - this is one giant set of 5 sets. do the first lift with designated reps then move on to the second lift, etc.
  • alternating dumbbell curls 8 (each arm)
  • skullcrushers 12
  • preacher curls 12-15
  • cable extensions 8-10
  • hammer curls with plate 10
  • tricep dips to failure
  • concentration curls 12
  • close grip bench with ez bar to failure (around 10)
week b
  • barbell curl warm-up with 30 then 4x8-10
  • alternating dumbbell curls and squeeze at top 12,10,8,15
  • preacher curls with cable rope 20,15,10,6,20
  • hammer curls with cable rope 20,15,10,6,20
  • tricep cable extension with rope warm-up with 30 then 4x8-10
  • dumbbell skullcrushers 4x10-12
  • french press with ez bar 4x6-8
  • single arm cable extension 3x12-15 superset with diamond pushups to failure
